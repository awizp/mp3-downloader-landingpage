About MP3 Downloader

A simple, open source tool that fetches high quality Tamil and regional audio tracks and saves them straight to your device fast and reliably.

The app runs from the client machine, it opens a terminal session where users can choose categories or paste source links.

The tool then converts and places downloaded MP3 files into a local output folder on the user's computer.

How It Works

Five simple steps follow in the terminal and get your songs.

Step 1

Open the Terminal

Open your system terminal and change to the downloader folder. Run the app to show the menu.

Step 2

Select a Category

Type the number of the category you want (e.g., Star Hits) and press Enter to confirm.

Step 3

Start Download

Choose a track number or type “all” to download. The app shows real time progress as it downloads and converts.

Step 4

Locate Output Folder

When finished, open the shown output folder ./output/<CategoryName>/<AlbumName>/ to access your MP3 files.

Step 5

Close the Terminal

Type exit or close the window. Your downloaded songs remain in the output folder.
